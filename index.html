Our Growth

There Are Behind The Auctus Group

RHMB Private Limited

RHMB Private Limited trades industrial metals and premium apparel with a focus on quality and reliability.

RHMB Private Limited is a diversified trading company engaged in the supply of industrial metals and apparel across India. The company specializes in trading TMT bars, metal products, and baby wear while delivering quality products through a reliable sourcing and distribution network. With a strong focus on efficiency, customer satisfaction, and long-term business relationships, RHMB continues to provide dependable trading solutions across multiple industry segments.

Jayesh Logistics Ltd.

Jayesh Logistics Ltd. delivers integrated B2B logistics and supply chain solutions across India and the India–Nepal trade corridor.

Jayesh Logistics Ltd. is a publicly listed logistics company delivering large-scale B2B transportation solutions across India with a strong specialization in the India–Nepal trade corridor. Serving the iron, steel, cement, and allied industries, the company provides reliable road freight, rail freight, port handling, and customs clearance services while maintaining the highest standards through ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 14001:2015 certifications.

Glocal Farms Private Limited

Glocal Farms Private Limited delivers infrastructure development and agricultural solutions across West Bengal.

Glocal Farms Private Limited is an infrastructure and agri-business company delivering sustainable development solutions across West Bengal. Specializing in irrigation systems, roads, bridges, tube wells, and water harvesting projects, the company supports rural and public infrastructure through reliable engineering and project execution.

Privin Project's Pvt Ltd.

Privin Projects Private Limited delivers civil construction and interior design solutions for modern infrastructure projects.

Privin Projects Private Limited is a leading construction and infrastructure company delivering high-quality civil construction and interior design solutions across India. With expertise in building development, hospital infrastructure, and public utility projects, the company is committed to creating sustainable, functional, and future-ready spaces through innovation, quality, and engineering excellence.

Ninetech Digital Services Private Limited

Ninetech Digital Services Private Limited delivers fintech (CMS, DMT, AEPS, bill payments) and digital technology solutions across India.

Ninetech Digital Services Private Limited is a fintech and technology company delivering secure digital banking and innovative software solutions across India. Focused on financial inclusion, the company provides rural banking services alongside website development, custom software, and mobile application solutions.

Prayatna Foundation is the designated Social Initiative Partner of the Auctus Group.

Prayatna grew out of a vision of minimizing social injustice towards women and children and empowering them with adequate education and training. The organization is at its nascent stage and aims at bettering the lives of the needful around. It believes in the global movement for Shiksha, Shakti, and Swasthya.
